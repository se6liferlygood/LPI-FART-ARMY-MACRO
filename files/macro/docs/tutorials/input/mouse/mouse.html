mouse buttons

you can click with C
for example:

C WF500 C2 C3

WF is wait flag
the script would click once then double click then triple click

you use the other mouse buttons the same way!
if no number is specified afterwards then it clicks once!

right click: CR
middle click (wheel button): CM

mouse movement

YOU GET SCREEN COORDINATE BY PRESSING CTRL ALT M
its shown as screen height and width mulitplied by a fraction

you can move the cursor with M(x,y)
note that you dont type x & y in fart army scripting like that!
I only meant x axis and y axis!

for example:

M(0,0)

would move the mouse to (0,0)
(0,0) IS LOCATED ON THE TOP LEFT CORNER OF YOUR SCREEN!

here are some usefull inbuilt variables that can be used in mouse movement:
screen height: ‡H‡
screen width: ‡W‡
mouse x: ‡X‡
mouse y: ‡Y‡

here are some examples where they can be used:

M(‡X‡,‡Y‡+10)

would move your cursor down by 10 pixels!
(because 0,0 is at the top left corner of your screen)

M(‡W‡/2,‡H‡/2)

would move your cursor to the center of your screen!

you can move & click at a coordinate by using MC(x,y)
instead of using M then C

MOUSE DRAG

x1,y1 is the starting point and what it drags
